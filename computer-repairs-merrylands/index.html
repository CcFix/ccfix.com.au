Computer repairs,
right here in Merrylands.

A local bench for local people — slow machines, broken screens, stubborn startups and everything in between.

If you're after somewhere nearby that actually takes the time to explain what's going on with your computer, you've found the right corner of Sydney. We're a small, local bench that looks after laptops and desktops for people right across Merrylands and the surrounding suburbs — and we like keeping it that way.

What we see most often

  • Machines that have slowed to a crawl over time
  • Viruses, pop-ups and other unwelcome software guests
  • Cracked screens, worn batteries and faulty keyboards
  • Computers that won't start, or take an age to do anything once they have
  • Lost files and the careful business of trying to bring them back

How we work through a job

  1. Diagnosis. We take a proper look and work out exactly what's going on — free of charge.
  2. The fix itself. Using quality parts and a careful hand, we put right what actually needs putting right.
  3. Testing. We run the machine through its paces to make sure everything's behaving as it should.
  4. Warranty. Every repair leaves the bench backed by our standard warranty — if something's not right, bring it back.

Out and about in the west

Along with Merrylands, we regularly see people from Parramatta, Guildford, Granville, Harris Park and Westmead — and plenty who simply prefer driving a little further for a shop that does things properly.
