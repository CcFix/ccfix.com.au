Common iPhone Battery Issues

1. Rapid Battery Drain

If your iPhone battery drains quickly, it could be due to software glitches, background apps, or an old battery that needs replacing.

2. Overheating

A battery that overheats can damage your iPhone. Replacing it promptly is essential.

3. Slow Charging

A worn-out battery may charge slowly. If your iPhone takes forever to charge, it’s time for a new battery.

4. Unexpected Shutdowns

If your iPhone shuts down even with a full charge, the battery likely needs replacing.
