iPhone repair
for Tallawong.

Cracked screen, flat battery or a port that won't charge — we fix iPhones properly, with quality parts and a warranty you can actually rely on.

If you're in Tallawong or anywhere across the northwest and your iPhone has seen better days, we're here to help. We're a small, careful repair bench in Sydney that looks after iPhones of all generations — from the older SE and 11 series right through to the latest models. No script, no upselling, just a straight answer about what's going on and a fair price to put it right.

What we repair most often

  • Cracked or broken screens — front glass, OLED and LCD replacements done properly, with parts that behave like the originals
  • Battery replacement — if your iPhone won't hold a charge through the day or shuts off unexpectedly, a new battery usually sorts it completely
  • Charging port repair — loose connection, lint packed in, or a port that's stopped working altogether
  • Back glass repair — cracked rear panels on iPhone 8 and later, fixed without replacing the whole phone
  • Camera faults — blurry photos, a black screen when you open the camera, or a lens that's been knocked out of place
  • Water damage assessment — if it's been dropped in water, bring it in quickly; earlier is always better

How the repair works

  1. Free diagnosis. We take a proper look at your iPhone and tell you exactly what's wrong — no charge for this, ever.
  2. Clear quote. You get a price before anything is touched. No surprises when you come to collect.
  3. The repair itself. We use quality parts and take our time — screens that calibrate properly, batteries that report accurate charge levels.
  4. Testing before handover. Touch response, Face ID, cameras, speakers — we check everything before we call it done.
  5. Warranty included. Every repair leaves our bench with a standard warranty. If something's not right, bring it back.

Serving Tallawong and the northwest

We regularly see people travelling in from Tallawong, Rouse Hill, Schofields, Riverstone, The Ponds, Stanhope Gardens, Kellyville, Marsden Park and Box Hill. If you're in the northwest corridor and want your iPhone fixed by someone who'll actually explain what they're doing, we're worth the trip.

Which iPhones do we work on?

All of them. iPhone SE, iPhone 8, the XR and XS range, iPhone 11 through to iPhone 15 and beyond — if Apple made it, we can fix it. Older models are equally welcome; a working older iPhone is often a better outcome than replacing it.
