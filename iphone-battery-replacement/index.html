Common questions

Before you call.

How do I know if my iPhone needs a new battery?

Common signs are rapid battery drain, the phone switching off well above 0%, or a "Service" message under Battery Health in Settings. If your Battery Health is sitting below 80%, a replacement usually makes a noticeable difference.

How long does an iPhone battery replacement take?

Most models are fitted the same day, often within the hour while you wait or run errands nearby.

Is there a warranty on the new battery?

Yes — every battery we fit is covered by a warranty on the part and the workmanship.

Is the replacement battery genuine Apple?

We fit premium aftermarket batteries that match or exceed the original capacity — new cells, not used or pulled parts. They're not Apple-branded, but they're built to the same capacity and safety standards.
