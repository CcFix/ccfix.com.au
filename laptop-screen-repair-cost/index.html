Laptops · Getting a quote

The simplest way to find
out what yours will need.

Rather than wade through rough averages that may not fit your laptop, here's the quickest route to an answer that actually applies to you.

Search around for laptop screen repair figures and you'll find a wide spread of numbers — most of them based on different brands, different panels, and different kinds of damage to the one sitting in front of you. None of that is much use when you're trying to work out what your laptop specifically needs.

What we do differently

  • We look at your actual laptop before saying anything definite
  • We explain what we find in plain terms — what's wrong, and why
  • We give you an honest, no-obligation quote on the spot
  • We only proceed once you're comfortable with what's involved

Two ways to get started

  1. Bring it in. No appointment needed — we'll take a look while you wait.
  2. Send us a description and photo. Tell us the brand, model and what happened, and we'll get back to you with an initial steer.

Straight answers, every time

If a repair doesn't make sense for your laptop, we'll tell you that too — plainly, and without any pressure to go ahead anyway.
