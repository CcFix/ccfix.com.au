Galaxy screen cracked?
Let's fix that.

Those curved, edge-to-edge AMOLED displays look stunning right up until a drop leaves them cracked or unresponsive — and they deserve a careful hand to put right.

Galaxy displays are some of the most striking on the market — vivid colour, deep contrast, and that signature curve at the edges. They're also more intricate to work on than a flat panel, which is exactly why it pays to have them handled carefully.

Signs your screen needs attention

  • Cracked or spider-webbed glass, especially near the curved edges
  • Patches of the display that won't respond to touch
  • Lines, flickering, or odd colours across the panel
  • A display that's dimmer or more washed-out than it used to be

How we handle it

We check exactly what's needed — sometimes it's the glass, sometimes the full display assembly — then fit a quality replacement and test touch response, colour, brightness and the curved edges before it goes back to you. Most common Galaxy models are done the same day.

Backed properly

A free diagnosis, an honest quote before we start, and a warranty on the finished repair — every time.
