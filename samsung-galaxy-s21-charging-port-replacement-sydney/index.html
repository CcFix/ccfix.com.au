Galaxy S21 · Charging · Sydney

Galaxy S21 charging
sorted properly.

A cable that won't sit flush, charging that drops out, or a port that's simply gone quiet — common things to see on an S21 that's been a daily companion for a while.

The S21 series remains one of the more popular Galaxy phones we see come through the door, and charging issues are one of the more common things they come in for. The good news: it's usually a tidy, contained repair.

What we look for

  • Whether the cable, the wall adapter, or the port itself is the actual problem
  • Lint, dust or moisture residue sitting inside the port
  • Bent or worn connector pins from repeated use
  • Whether charging speed has dropped off compared to when it was new

How we carry out the repair

If the port needs replacing, we fit one suited specifically to the S21, taking care with the surrounding components — antennas, seals and the board connections all sit close by. Once it's done, we test charging speed, stability and data connection before handing it back.
