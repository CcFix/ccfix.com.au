HP Laptops · What it costs

Sorting out an
HP laptop screen.

From everyday Pavilions to premium Spectres and EliteBooks, HP covers a huge range — and the right approach to a screen repair depends on exactly which one you've got.

HP makes laptops at every price point, and the screens inside them vary just as widely — from straightforward standard panels to higher-end touch and high-resolution displays. There's no single answer that fits the whole range, which is exactly why we look before we quote.

What shapes the job on an HP laptop

  • Whether it's an everyday Pavilion, a slimline Envy, or a premium Spectre or EliteBook
  • Standard, touch or high-resolution panel types
  • How the damage occurred — and whether the lid or hinges were involved
  • Whether the laptop is still under any existing cover that's worth checking first

Our approach

We open it up, see exactly what your particular HP needs, fit a quality replacement panel, and test the display thoroughly — brightness, colour, touch response where applicable — before handing it back.

Get a straight figure

Bring it in, or tell us the model and what happened. We'll take a proper look and give you an honest, instant quote — no guesswork, no surprises.
