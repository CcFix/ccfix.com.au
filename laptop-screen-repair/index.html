Laptops · Screens

Laptop screen repair,
done properly.

Cracked glass, flickering panels, lines across the display, or a screen that's gone dark — whatever the laptop, the same careful process applies.

A laptop screen takes more daily wear than people realise — opened and closed hundreds of times, carried in bags, occasionally bumped on the way out the door. When something finally gives, we're here to put it right properly, not just patch it over.

Signs your screen needs attention

  • Cracked, chipped or spider-webbed glass
  • Flickering, lines, or patches of odd colour across the display
  • A picture that's dim, washed out, or has gone dark altogether
  • Touch response that's stopped working in places (on touch-screen models)

How we handle it

We open the laptop up carefully, work out exactly what's needed — sometimes the panel alone, sometimes the wider assembly — fit a quality replacement matched to your model, and test brightness, colour and touch response thoroughly before it goes back to you.

What you'll get

A free diagnosis, an honest quote before any work begins, careful fitting, and a warranty on the finished repair. Most common models are done quickly — often while you wait nearby.
