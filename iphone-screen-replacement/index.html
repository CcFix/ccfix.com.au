Common questions

Before you call.

What's the difference between Incell and Soft OLED replacement screens?

Incell is a high quality aftermarket panel with accurate colour and full touch response at a lower price point. Soft OLED is a premium panel that reproduces the deep blacks, contrast and brightness of the factory display — the closest match if you want that original feel back.

How long does an iPhone screen replacement take?

Most models are fitted the same day, often within the hour while you wait or run errands nearby.

Is there a warranty on the new screen?

Yes — every screen we fit, Incell or Soft OLED, is covered by a warranty on the parts and the workmanship.

Will Face ID and the front camera still work after the repair?

We test Face ID, the front camera and True Tone on every device before it leaves the bench.
