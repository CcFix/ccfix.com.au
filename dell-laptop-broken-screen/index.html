Dell Laptops · Screens

Dell laptop screen
taken a knock?

From everyday Inspirons to premium XPS machines and rugged Latitudes, Dell covers a lot of ground — and we see plenty of each on the bench.

Dell laptops are everywhere — homes, offices, schools — and that means we get a steady stream of them in for screen repairs. Each range has its own quirks, from how the lid is put together to the type of panel fitted inside, so it pays to have someone who's familiar with them.

What we keep an eye on with Dell laptops

  • Hinges that can be put under extra strain when a lid takes a knock
  • A mix of standard, touch and high-resolution panels across different ranges
  • Webcams and microphones often built into the display bezel itself
  • Older Dell models that are still very much worth keeping going

How we handle it

We open the laptop carefully, confirm exactly what's been affected — sometimes it's just the panel, sometimes the hinges or bezel too — fit a quality replacement matched to your model, and test it thoroughly before handing it back.

What you can expect

An honest look, a clear quote before we start, and a tidy result you can rely on — backed by our standard warranty.
